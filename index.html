Our Solutions

Cartridges

1,500 to 3,000 cartridges per hour

Our RoboCAP RL-200, RL-300 & RL-500 Volumetric (VF) systems are engineered to maximize efficiency in low-volume product filling (up to 5ml), achieving a precise +/- 2.5% filling accuracy through advanced backpressure, heating, and volumetric displacement technologies.

Gummies

1,000 to 4,000 gummies per hour

For high-viscosity, time-sensitive products like gummies, an accurate and efficient filling system is essential to optimize production. The RoboCAP RL-300, paired with the Volumetric Filling Deluxe (VFD) nozzle, is designed to meet your precise production requirements.

Bottles

1,200 to 3,000 bottles per hour

The RoboCAP RL-300 Peristaltic System makes bottle filling easier than ever. This pressure-free system minimizes product contact for quick changeovers and easy cleaning.

Capsules

3,000 to 8,000 capsules per hour

The RoboCAP RL-300 Volumetric (VF) Nozzle makes capsule filling simpler and more efficient than ever. Specifically designed for low-volume filling, the VF Nozzle delivers capsules with a filling accuracy of +/- 2.5%.

Creams & Topicals

500 to 1,500 units per hour

Our filling solution seamlessly integrates pneumatic and peristaltic components to deliver an optimal lotion filling system. Designed to handle even the thickest lotions and balms.

Balms

600 to 1,500 units per hour

Filling your balms has never been easier than with the RoboCAP RL-300 Pneumatic Nozzle. This system harnesses air pressure and a larger dispense needle to ensure precise and consistent filling.
